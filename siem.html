The Sandip Institute of Engineering and Management (SIEM) is an esteemed educational institution located in Nashik, Maharashtra, India. It is affiliated with the Savitribai Phule Pune University (SPPU) and approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE). SIEM strives to provide quality education and holistic development opportunities to its students.
